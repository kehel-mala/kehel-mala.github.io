## 🍌 කෙහෙල් මල 🍌 kehel mala
[_Sri Lankan slang, from **kehel** banana + **mala** flower_]

_Colloquially used to mean something along the lines of "Ah shit!", "Da hell!" or to express strong contradiction, similar to saying "Yeah, right!" or "Mhmm" (with attitude)._

Usage: When your sister does some dumb shit, you say, "_Mona kehel malakda?!_" to convey your displeasure at them.
